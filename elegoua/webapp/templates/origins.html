-- Our problematic ---------------------------

In 2021, more than 12 million(1) students entered schools, colleges and high schools in France. It is also 7.8%(2) of early school leavers, ie of students who have dropped out of school and have no qualifications or diplomas.

One of the major factors in this abandonment of school lies in the academic difficulty which is all the more reinforced in middle school where more than 40%(3) of pupils experience repetition. The teaching methods are called into question because they are considered to be not very rewarding and not very differentiated. Yet each student has different educational needs.

How to overcome these difficulties and best support students in their journey? How to fight against early exit from the school system, depriving some of a diploma which is nevertheless essential for job search?

-- Birth of the Elegoua Project ---------------

Elegoua was born from the desire to make learning simple and suitable for everyone. While some learn better with examples, others prefer to go straight to the point and still others need diagrams to understand... Everyone's needs are different, so why offer the same course for everyone ?

-- Origin of the name Elegoua ---------------

In the Yoruba religion ("the source of our tradition"), in the South West of Nigeria, Eleggua is the spirit of communication, intermediary between divinities and men. He is the divine messenger and great negotiator between the negative forces and the positive forces of the body. In the same way, the Elegoua project acts as an intermediary between knowledge and yourself.
